Rice:

Wash and cook rice according to your preferred method (stovetop, rice cooker, or instant pot).
Sambar:

Cook tuvar dal (pigeon peas lentil) until soft. Mash it well.
In a separate pot, prepare the sambar by cooking vegetables, tamarind pulp, sambar powder, and cooked dal together.
Season with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and asafoetida in oil.
Let the vegetables cook until tender and the flavors meld.
Rasam:

Prepare the rasam base using tomatoes, tamarind, rasam powder, and spices.
Season with mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves, and asafoetida in oil.
Simmer the rasam until it's flavorful and slightly tangy.
Vegetable Curry and Poriyal:

Choose vegetables of your choice and chop them into bite-sized pieces.
Saut√© mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaves, and asafoetida in oil.
Add vegetables and cook until tender, seasoning with turmeric, salt, and optional grated coconut.
Coconut Chutney:

Blend grated coconut, roasted chana dal (split chickpeas), green chilies, ginger, and a little water until smooth.
Season with mustard seeds, urad dal, curry leaves, and asafoetida in oil.
Curd:

Serve plain yogurt as is or whisk it with a bit of salt.
Papadum:

Roast or fry papadums until crispy.
